A strange bird that called an island home, it went extinct when invaders from strange lands overtook its home,
unable to fight back. These birds have an affinity for melons and pumpkins, and they love pecking at them as a form of
enrichment, making them useful for automating melon and pumpkin farms.
<NEWLINE>
Every once in a while they may also drop some of their feathers, making them a good source of them.
